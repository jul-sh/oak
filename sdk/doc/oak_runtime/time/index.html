MicrosSinceEpoch

Time is given as microseconds since the UNIX epoch (00:00:00 UTC on 1 January 1970). Leap seconds are linearly smeared over a 24-hour period. That is, the smear extends from UTC noon to noon over 86,401 or 86,399 SI seconds, and all the smeared seconds are the same length.
